About Zaheed

About Zaheed

An electronic platform that aggregates offers and discounts for products and services from various retail stores, serving as a link between them and their customers.

The aim is to empower merchants to reach a larger segment of consumers and market their various offers, while simultaneously providing consumers with the opportunity to know the latest offers and make direct purchases to get the best value!

Our Ambition

To be the primary reference showcasing offers and discounts to ensure financial savings for consumers, while promoting retail offers to achieve high sales.

Our Mission

To showcase offers and discounts for store products and enable consumers to make direct purchases and obtain better value.

How to Use the Zaheed Application

1. View offers from store pages.
2. Select products from various retail stores.
3. Make a direct purchase.
4. Receive the order.
5. The customer enjoys getting the best value!
